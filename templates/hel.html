Help & Support

Introduction

Welcome to the Sign Language Detection web page. This tool allows you to detect sign language gestures using your webcam. You can choose between American Sign Language (ASL) and Indian Sign Language (ISL) for detection.

How to Use

  • Ensure your webcam is connected and working.
  • Allow the web page to access your camera when prompted.
  • Select either the "AMERICAN" or "INDIAN" button to start sign language detection for the respective language.
  • The video feed will appear on the left side, and detected signs will be displayed in the output box on the right side.
  • To stop the detection, click the "STOP" button.

Requirements

  • A working webcam.
  • Permission to access your camera.
  • A stable internet connection.
  • A modern web browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, etc.).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What should I do if the camera is not working?

Ans: Make sure your webcam is connected and that you have allowed the web page to access it. Try refreshing the page or restarting your browser.

Q: The detection is not accurate. What can I do?

Ans: Ensure you are in a well-lit environment and your hand gestures are clear and within the camera's view.

Q: Can I use this on my mobile device?

Ans: Yes, as long as your mobile device has a camera and you use a compatible browser.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • If the camera feed does not appear, try refreshing the page and granting camera access again.
  • Ensure there is enough lighting in the room for the camera to detect your gestures clearly.
  • If the detection is slow, check your internet connection and close any unnecessary applications or tabs.
